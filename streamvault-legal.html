FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions.

How do downloads work?

Simply paste a valid media URL into the Downloader tab. StreamVault PRO will analyze the link and offer various quality options. Once selected, the file is securely retrieved and sent directly to your device.

How do conversions work?

Navigate to the Converter tab and select a local file. You can choose to convert videos to MP4/MP3 or audio to MP3 with custom quality settings. Our high-speed engine processes the file natively.

Where are files saved?

We value your privacy. Files are processed temporarily on our secure backend and immediately transferred to your local browser download manager. We do not permanently store your downloaded media on our servers.

How do subscriptions work?

We offer Free and Premium tiers. Premium tiers unlock features like ad-free experiences, 4K downloads, and unlimited batch processing. Subscriptions can be managed directly from your Profile settings.

Common Troubleshooting

- Download fails: Ensure the URL is public and valid.
- Conversion error: Verify the original file is not corrupted.
- Login issues: Clear your browser cookies and try again.
